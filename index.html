Our Story

Founded in 2010 by master pizzaiolo Antonio Rossi, Slice Heaven brings authentic Neapolitan pizza to your neighborhood. After years of perfecting his craft in Naples, Antonio brought his family recipes and wood-fired oven techniques to create the perfect pizza experience.

We use only the finest ingredients - imported Italian "00" flour, San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella di bufala, and premium extra virgin olive oil. Our dough is hand-stretched and fermented for 48 hours to achieve the perfect texture and flavor.
